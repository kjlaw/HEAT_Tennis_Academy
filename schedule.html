May 10
3:00pm - 5:00pm at Cranbrook
Cost for this kick-off event is $30. Everyone who comes to the kick-off gets a free H.E.A.T. bag tag and a free H.E.A.T. t-shirt. Rain day for the kick-off is May 11, 3:00pm - 5:00pm.

June 2 - June 6
4:00pm - 6:30pm at Cranbrook
We welcome all players to come out during this time. We realize that most of you are still in school and battling final exams. We would encourage you to come out and clear your heads for a couple hours while maintaining your timing and rhythm with your tennis game. For those of you who are preparing for the qualifier, we will be offering an intense 2 1/2 hour training session focused on live ball drills and point play.

June 9 - June 13
8:00am - 10:30am at Cranbrook
4:00pm - 6:30pm at Cranbrook
Both sessions are open to all players of all levels. For those of you who are out of school, we welcome you to come to either session. For those of you still in school, we would encourage you to come to the afternoon session.

9:00am - 11:30am at Seaholm

11:30am - 2:00pm at Cranbrook
