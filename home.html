Welcome to H.E.A.T. Tennis Academy's Summer Camp 2014!

"Tennis demands you to achieve a balance between a healthy mind and a healthy body. At the H.E.A.T. Academy we strive to instill that balance in each one of our students as we mold our participants into skilled tennis players and top-notch people. Tennis is not just a game to us at H.E.A.T., it is a way of life."

H.E.A.T. is the perfect fit for all levels of tennis players. Future stars, high school players, and tournament players will improve from a high energy experience. Our camp focuses on footwork, consistency, technique, mental training and point development for both singles and doubles. Our staff has more experience and depth than any other summer program. H.E.A.T. uses fun and innovative instruction to challenge every player to go beyond their limitations.

Please explore our website to find all the information regarding our new summer program. We look forward to seeing you on the courts this summer to come feel the H.E.A.T.!
